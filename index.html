Bubblegoose is now live on Bsc

The Bubblegoose are 10,000 uniquely generated characters. No two are exactly alike, and each one of them can be officially owned by a single person on the Ethereum blockchain. Originally, they could be claimed for free by anybody with an Ethereum wallet, but all 10,000 were quickly claimed. Now they must be purchased from someone via the marketplace that's also embedded in the blockchain. we introduced the BubbleGoose coin to facilitate transactions between goose nft holders and normal transactions on the binance smart chain.
